A little about JoJo's recipie book

We are thrilled to introduce our Nepali Cuisine Recipe Book, a delightful collection of authentic recipes that capture the rich flavors and vibrant traditions of Nepal! Featuring both timeless classics and modern twists, this book celebrates the essence of Nepali culture—from hearty dal bhat and flavorful momo to sweet treats like sel roti and yomari.
This recipe book was inspired by the spirit of preserving family recipes and cultural heritage, first shared during Nepali New Year celebrations. We’re overjoyed to extend this culinary journey to you, bringing the warmth and hospitality of Nepali kitchens straight to your table.
